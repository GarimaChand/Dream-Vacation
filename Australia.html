Australia

Australia is one of the most beautiful and amazing places in the world. There are a lot of things in Australia that can amaze anyone! from Sydney Opera House to the best beaches in the world, from the largest and most beautiful reef on the planet to the coolest animals like Kangaroos and koalas.

This is the Sydney Opera House in Australia.It is situated on Bennelong Point in Sydney Harbor and close to the Sydney Harbor Bridge.The Opera House was constructed by about 10,000 workers and was opened by Queen Elizabeth II on 20th October, 1973. The Sydney Opera House is the most distinctive landmark in Sydney.

The picture above is coral reef located in Australia.It is located in the Coral sea, off the coast of Queensland, Australia. It is called the Great Barrier Reef and one of the world's largest coral reef.The Great Barrier Reef can be seen from outer space and is the world's biggest single structure made by living organisms. This place is one the best places to visit in Australia.

The image if the beach above is called Noosa Main Beach. It is the also one of the best places to visit and relax during the summer. The image above is the morning sunrise view of the beach. It looks really preety :)
